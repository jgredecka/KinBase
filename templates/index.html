About KinBase


KinBase is a free and public data repository for kinase-related data. The mission of KinBase is to provide the scientific community with an easily accessible resource of kinase, commercial kinase inhibitor and phosphosite information.


KinBase offers three main types of search:

Kinases: for searching human kinases of choice using HGNC-approved gene symbols.
Inhibitors: for searching commercial kinase inhibitors using HGNC-approved gene symbols.
Phosphosites: for browsing phosphosites according to specific parameters.

Acknowledgements


KinBase was developed by a group of post-graduate students as part of the Bioinformatics MSc programme at Queen Mary University of London under the supervision of Prof Conrad Bessant and Dr Fabrizio Smeraldi.
